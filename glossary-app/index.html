    Interactive Glossary¶

    Welcome to the interactive glossary for AI for Investor Relations Transformation. This enhanced interface provides multiple ways to explore and understand the 298 concepts covered in this course.

    Features¶

    • Smart Search: Search across terms, definitions, and examples
    • Multiple Views: Browse alphabetically, by category, or follow learning paths
    • Learning Paths: Organized progression from beginner to advanced concepts
    • Smart Filtering: Filter by difficulty level, category, or first letter
    • Core Concepts: Highlighted foundational concepts essential for understanding

    Navigation¶

    Alphabetical View¶

    Browse all terms in alphabetical order with A-Z filtering. Click any term to expand and see the full definition and examples.

    By Category View¶

    Explore concepts organized into 12 thematic categories: - Transformation & Strategy: Change management and strategic planning - Analytics & Predictions: Data analysis and forecasting - Agentic AI Systems: Autonomous AI workflows - Regulatory Compliance: Legal and compliance requirements - Valuation Metrics: Financial performance indicators - Data Governance: Data quality and security - AI Governance: Responsible AI practices - AI Content Creation: AI-generated communications - AI Technology: Core AI concepts and tools - IR Foundations: Investor relations fundamentals - IR Operations: Day-to-day IR activities - Investor Types: Understanding different investor segments

    Learning Path View¶

    Follow a structured learning progression: 1. Core Concepts: Essential foundations (9 concepts) 2. Beginner: Fundamental concepts with no prerequisites (152 concepts) 3. Intermediate: Practical applications (89 concepts) 4. Advanced: Expert-level topics (52 concepts)

    Alternative View¶

    If you prefer the traditional format, you can view the simple glossary with all terms in a single scrollable page.

    About This Glossary¶

    All definitions follow ISO 11179 metadata standards, ensuring they are: - Precise: Accurately captures meaning - Concise: 20-50 words (average 38 words) - Distinct: Unique definitions - Non-circular: No circular references - Free of business rules: States what it IS, not what you should do

    Quality Metrics¶

    • Total Terms: 298 concepts
    • Example Coverage: 96% (287 terms with examples)
    • Categories: 12 thematic groupings
    • Difficulty Levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced
    • Core Concepts: 9 foundational terms
    • Version: 2.0 (Generated: 2025-11-06)

    How to Use¶

    1. Search First: Use the search box to quickly find specific terms
    2. Explore Categories: Browse related concepts within thematic groups
    3. Follow Learning Paths: Progress systematically from beginner to advanced
    4. Study Core Concepts: Start with the 9 essential foundations
    5. Use Examples: Each term includes real-world applications

    Integration with Course¶

    This glossary is fully integrated with: - Learning Graph - Visual concept dependencies - FAQ - Common questions and detailed explanations - Course chapters - Concepts linked throughout content

    Tip: Click the category tags on any term to see related concepts in the same domain.
