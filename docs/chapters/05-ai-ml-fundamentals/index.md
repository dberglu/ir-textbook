# AI and Machine Learning Fundamentals

## Summary

This chapter introduces the foundational concepts of artificial intelligence and machine learning that enable modern IR transformation. You'll learn AI fundamentals, machine learning basics, how large language models (LLMs) work, the capabilities of generative AI tools, and essential prompt engineering skills for working with AI systems. The chapter also covers AI governance models, developing AI policies, responsible AI practices, and AI ethics for finance. This foundational AI knowledge is essential for all subsequent chapters on AI-powered IR applications, from content creation to predictive analytics to agentic systems.
